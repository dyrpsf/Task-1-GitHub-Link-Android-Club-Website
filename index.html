What we do at the club

Our Mission

Empower students to craft delightful Android experiences through peer learning, mentorship, and real-world projects. We focus on fundamentals, clean architecture, design systems, and shipping to users.

120+Active Members
25+Projects
15Workshops/Yr

What you’ll learn

  • Modern Android with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose
  • UI/UX fundamentals and motion design
  • Clean architecture, testing, and CI/CD
  • Publishing to Play Store and open-source collaboration
  • Cloud backends with Firebase and REST APIs
